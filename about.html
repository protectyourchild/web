About Us

Our team started this website to help better inform Filipinos about "Preventive Ways Ranging Childhood Diseases".



We are students from Our Lady of Fatima University whose goal is to share information about Childhood Diseases and ways to fight them. Our team believe that parents should be aware and should take action with the threats surrounding their children.



Our team gathered information about topics that people find hard to understand. This website will serve as a platform where we made learning easier.



By this website, we push the bounderies of learning and employ the essence of change throughout everyone. Because we believe that innovation will keep making lives better.
